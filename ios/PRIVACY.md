# RealTime Space — Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** July 8, 2026

RealTime Space is built to be enjoyed, not to collect your data.

## What we collect

**Nothing.** RealTime Space does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or usage data. There are no accounts, no sign-ins, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking of any kind.

## What stays on your device

- Your exploration progress (scanned objects, missions, settings) is stored only on your device.
- The date you first launched the app is stored locally to run the 60-day free period.

None of this ever leaves your phone.

## Purchases

The optional one-time "Unlock Forever" purchase is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment information. Purchases can be restored on any device signed into the same Apple Account.

## Network use

The app works fully offline. The only network connection it makes is to Apple's StoreKit service for the optional purchase described above.

## Children

RealTime Space is safe for all ages. Because we collect no data at all, no data is collected from children.

## Changes

If this policy ever changes, the update will be posted at this page.
