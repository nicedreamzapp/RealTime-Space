# Privacy Policy — RealTime Space (Android & iOS)

**Effective date: July 13, 2026**

RealTime Space is built by Nice Dreamz LLC ("we", "us"). This policy covers the RealTime Space mobile app on Android and iOS.

## The short version

**RealTime Space does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Period.**

The entire universe — all planets, moons, and 8,920 real stars — is bundled with the app and rendered on your device. There are no accounts, no ads, and no tracking of any kind.

## Details

- **No accounts**: The app has no sign-up, no login, and no user profiles.
- **No analytics or ads**: The app contains no advertising SDKs, no trackers, and no analytics libraries.
- **Offline by design**: All 3D graphics, star catalogs, and audio are bundled with the app and work with no connection.
- **Optional live space data**: If your device is online, the app may request public, anonymous astronomy data — the current position of the International Space Station (wheretheiss.at) and NASA's public near-Earth-object feed (api.nasa.gov) — purely to display it in the app. These requests contain no personal information, no identifiers, and nothing about you. If you are offline, the app simply skips them.
- **No data sale**: We have nothing to sell, because we collect nothing.

## Permissions

RealTime Space requests no sensitive permissions — no camera, no microphone, no location, no contacts.

## Children

The app collects no data from anyone, including children.

If this policy ever changes, we will update this page and note the new effective date above.
